Page 6 = Helpline Information

These are the helplines for different kinds of situation that you may be facing. Use them anytime if you feel anxious, or if you just want someone to listen to you.

Helplines:

  • Kids HelpLine - Telephone counselling service for 5-25 year olds - 1800 55 1800 - Operates 24/7
  • Headspace - Mental health support for young people aged 12-25 - 1800 650 890 - 10pm to 1am
  • Directline - For people impacted by drug use - 1800 888 236 - Operates 24/7
  • Griefline - Support for experiencing loss and grief - 1300 845 745 - Noon to 3am
  • Lifeline - Crisis support and suicide prevention - 13 11 14 - Operates 24/7
  • Beyondblue - Depression, anxiety and related disorders - 1300 22 4636 - Operates 24/7
